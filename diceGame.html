DICE-DRINKING-GAME!

5'S, 10'S, 15'S, 20'S

Instructions

1. Right click/inspect/console. (ctrl/shift/i/console).
2. Player rolls all the dice at the same time.
3. First, the player will add up the total of all the dice.
4. Whatever number is rolled on the 4 sided die = number of seconds you drink your kool aid for.
5. If a 5, 10, 15 or 20 are rolled on the 20 sided die then->
6. 5 = player who rolled, drinks kool aid for the amount of seconds rolled on the 4 die and you loose all your points.
7. 10 = player gets to pick another player to drink kool aid for x amount of seconds.
8. 15 = everyone else drinks kool aid for x amount of seconds.
9. 20 = player gets to pick another player to drink kool aid for x amount of seconds and that player also looses all their points.
10. Players only get one turn per round and play until someone gets 100 points.
